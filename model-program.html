Common Questions

Got Questions?

In the Model Program, you earn a percentage of the ad revenue generated by your verified videos. Your payout depends on ad performance — views, clicks, viewer geography, and ad rates all factor in.

Example average RPMs: 2018 — $0.64 | 2017 — $0.69 | 2016 — $0.62 | 2015 — $0.44 per 1,000 views.

On ModelHub: you keep 65% of video sales and 80% of tips and fan club subscriptions.
We pay out 80%+ of the ad revenue your videos earn. Your earnings are based on views multiplied by the ad rate. The ad rate reflects the performance of ads around your video — including clicks, user country, and sales conversions.

On ModelHub, the 65% revenue share is 80% of your net revenue after payment processing fees are deducted.
Payments are made monthly with a minimum withdrawal of $100 USD. If you earn less than $100 in a month, it rolls over to the next month.

Payouts are processed in the first week of every month. Paxum payments typically arrive by the 2nd or 3rd week; checks by the 3rd week. You can update your payment method in your settings at any time before month-end.
Videos are only eligible for earnings if you appear in them. You must confirm this during the upload process. If there are other performers, you need a co-performer agreement on file — we may request it at any time.

If you forgot to confirm during upload, email us the video link at models@streamvault.com and we'll update it.
Your earnings tab breaks down: Free Video Earnings (ad rev), Viewshare Earnings, For Sale Video Earnings, Other Earnings (tips, fan clubs, custom videos), and your Total Month and All-Time Earnings.

Earnings are recalculated daily — check back often. Pending earnings are estimates and may fluctuate slightly until finalized.
